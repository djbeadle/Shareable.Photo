Features

  • Upload from any device iPhone, Android, desktop, and more!
  • Files aren't compressed and image metadata is preserved
  • Most commonly supported image and video formats are supported by default(.jpg, .png, .mp4, .mov, etc). Uncommon filetypes can be supported upon request.
  • Due to limitations in iOS Live Photos (.heic) from Apple mobile devices will be converted to still JPG files before upload when being uploaded from the Camera Roll.

F.A.Q.

Is uploaded media encrypted?
No. System administrators can view uploaded media to prevent abuse of this service.
Are uploaders anonymous?
Uploaders do not have to create an account to upload media but IP addresses are logged.
